Foodzone works in any browser as long as you have a connection
to the internet. You can access your info on any computer or device,

Easily share food diaries with other people, saving time avoiding printing

people to collaborate on one diary without having to upload or export
anything.

Easily share in depth reports as PDF.

Several in depth reports can be made and exported to PDF or excel.
The nutrient report shows the individuals nutrient intake over a
selected date range for each of the NRV areas including Estimated
Average Requirements (EAR), Recommended Dietary Intake (RDI),
Average Intake (AI), Suggested Daily Targets (SDT), Upper Limits
(UL), Macronutrients as a percentage of energy intake and Fatty
acids as a percentage of fat intake.
